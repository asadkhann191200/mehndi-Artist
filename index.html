Meet the Artist Behind the Art

Hello! Welcome to Shifa Mehndi Art. My name is Shifa, and I am a professional Mehndi artist with a deep passion for turning hands into beautiful, living canvases.

For me, Mehndi is not just a service; it is a beautiful tradition that marks the most joyous moments of a woman's life. I have perfected the art of creating customized, flawless designs that reflect your personality.

I pride myself on using high-quality, chemical-free henna that guarantees a rich, dark stain and is completely safe for your skin. Let’s make your special moments unforgettable, together.

Our Services

Bridal Mehndi
